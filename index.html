Stories and STIX2

CTI is very much about telling stories. Information becomes intelligence when it is complimented with the context and is placed in the story. These stories are usually crystalised in the reports. If intelligence provider dissaminating the reports cares about structural CTI, these reports will be supplemeted with STIX2 bundles.

There is a gap there between a story, narrated in the report, and a structured CTI snapshot, represented by STIX2 bundle. I believe we can combine these, making the stories more informative, interactive and engaging.

The objective of stixview library is to provide easily embeddable STIX2 graphs, providing necessary level of interactivity.
